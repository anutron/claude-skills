## Use /airon-develop Instead of /brainstorm

When the superpowers:brainstorming trigger fires (creative work, new features, building components, adding functionality, modifying behavior), use `/airon-develop` instead of `/brainstorm` or `superpowers:brainstorming`. The `/airon-develop` skill wraps brainstorming with a tighter review flow — one gate (the plan) instead of two (spec + plan).

**This applies to all projects in `~/Personal/*` and `~/Development/ai/*`.**
